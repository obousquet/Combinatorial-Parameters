Diameter

Name: Diameter

Symbol: $\mathrm{D}$

Definition:

The diameter $D(\mathcal{H})$ of a family $\mathcal{H}$ is the largest distance between two elements of the family, i.e. $$ D(\mathcal{H}):= \max_{h_1,h_2\in\mathcal{H}} |h_1\Delta h_2| $$

Comments:

The diameter is equal to the strict diameter dimension.

Category: Basic

Symmetric: Yes

Monotonic: Yes

P-Monotonic: Yes

Doubly Monotonic: Yes

Strictly Monotonic: No

Values:

  • Diameter of Trivial concepts
  • Diameter of Tagged Singletons

Relationships (as Parameter 1):

  • Diameter / Path Dimension
  • Diameter / Oriented Diameter
  • Diameter / Hamming Radius

Relationships (as Parameter 2):

  • Effective Range / Diameter
  • Oriented Diameter / Diameter
  • Hamming Radius / Diameter
