Effective VC Radius

Name: Effective VC Radius

Symbol: $\mathrm{VCR}$

Definition:

For a family $\mathcal{H}\subset 2^\mathcal{X}$, we define the effective VC radius as the largest integer $k$ such that all subsets of size $k$ of the effective range of $\mathcal{H}$, i.e. $\bigcup {h\Delta h': h,h'\in \mathcal{H}}$ are shattered by $\mathcal{H}$.

Category: Shattering

Symmetric: Yes

Monotonic: Yes

P-Monotonic: No

Doubly Monotonic: No

Strictly Monotonic: No

Values:

  • Effective VC Radius of Singletons
  • Effective VC Radius of Trivial concepts

Relationships (as Parameter 1): None

Relationships (as Parameter 2):

  • VC Dimension / Effective VC Radius

  • Minimum Teaching Set Size / Effective VC Radius
  • Projected VC Radius / Effective VC Radius
  • co-VC dimension / Effective VC Radius
  • Recursive Teaching Dimension / Effective VC Radius
