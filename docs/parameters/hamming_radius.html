Hamming Radius

Name: Hamming Radius

Symbol: $\mathrm{B}$

Definition:

The Hamming Radius $B(\mathcal{H})$ of a family $\mathcal{H}$ is the smallest radius of an enclosing Hamming ball, defined as $$ B(\mathcal{H}):= \min{d: \exists T\subset \mathcal{X},\, \forall h\in \mathcal{H},\, |h\Delta T|\le d} $$
