Log Shattering

Name: Log Shattering

Symbol: $\log \mathrm{sh}$

Definition:

The Log Shattering of a family $\mathcal{H}$ is the (binary) log of the number of its shattered sets, $$ \log |sh{\mathcal{H|}} $$

Category: Shattering

Symmetric: Yes

Monotonic: Yes

P-Monotonic: Yes

Doubly Monotonic: Yes

Strictly Monotonic: Yes

Values:

  • Log Shattering of Trivial concepts
  • Log Shattering of Singletons

Relationships (as Parameter 1):

  • Log Shattering / Log Size
  • Log Shattering / Effective Range
  • Log Shattering / Majority Complexity

Relationships (as Parameter 2):

  • VC Dimension / Log Shattering
  • Effective Range / Log Shattering
