Maximum Degree

Name: Maximum Degree

Symbol: $\mathrm{d}_{\max}$

Definition:

The maximum degree of a concept class is defined as the maximum degree of its $1$-inclusion graph.

Category: Graph-based

Symmetric: Yes

Monotonic: Yes

P-Monotonic: No

Doubly Monotonic: No

Strictly Monotonic: No

Values:

  • Maximum Degree of Tagged Singletons
  • Maximum Degree of Addressing
  • Maximum Degree of Majority
  • Maximum Degree of Half-intervals
  • Maximum Degree of Singletons plus empty set
  • Maximum Degree of Full Cube
  • Maximum Degree of Singletons
  • Maximum Degree of Trivial concepts

Relationships (as Parameter 1):

  • Maximum Degree / Double Density or Average Degree
  • Maximum Degree / Minimum Degree
  • Maximum Degree / Degeneracy
  • Maximum Degree / Densest Subgraph (twice)
  • Maximum Degree / Maximum Positive Degree
  • Maximum Degree / Largest Strongly Shattered Set

Relationships (as Parameter 2):

  • Projected Maximal Degree / Maximum Degree
